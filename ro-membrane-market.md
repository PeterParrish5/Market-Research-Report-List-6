<p><strong>The RO Membrane Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Composite Membranes</li><li>Asymmetric Membranes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The reverse osmosis (RO) membrane market is primarily categorized into composite membranes and asymmetric membranes. Composite membranes are made of multiple layers that enhance filtration efficiency and chemical resistance, making them ideal for various applications. Asymmetric membranes, on the other hand, have a dense selective layer on a porous support structure, allowing for high permeability while maintaining selectivity. Both types are essential in water purification, seawater desalination, and industrial processes, addressing different performance and cost requirements.</p></p>
