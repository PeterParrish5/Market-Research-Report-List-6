<p><p>Coloured concrete can be categorized into two main types: synthetic and natural. Synthetic coloured concrete is made by adding pigments or dyes to the concrete mix, providing a wide range of vibrant colours and customization options. Natural coloured concrete, on the other hand, utilizes natural mineral pigments such as iron oxide to achieve earthy tones like browns and reds. Both types of coloured concrete offer durability, versatility, and aesthetic appeal, making them popular choices for a variety of construction and landscaping projects.</p></p>
