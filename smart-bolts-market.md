<p><p>Smart bolts are available in various market types based on their head designs and usage. Hex bolts are the most common type with a hexagonal head for easy installation and removal. Flange bolts have a built-in washer to distribute pressure and prevent loosening. Socket bolts have a recessed head for tamper resistance. Stud bolts are fully threaded with no head, used with nuts for secure fastening. Other market types include specialty bolts like square head bolts or carriage bolts, each designed for specific applications.</p></p>
