<p><p>The stainless steel gratings market consists of several types, including welded grating, swage locked grating, press locked grating, and others. Welded grating features individual bars welded together, providing strength and durability. Swage locked grating uses a pressing method to lock the bearing bars into the cross bars, ensuring a sturdy construction. Press locked grating involves mechanically locking the bars without welding, offering a lightweight yet robust option. Other types may include custom or specialized designs to meet specific industrial requirements.</p></p>
