<p><strong>The Semiconductor Etchants Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wet Etching Agent</li><li>Dry Etching Agent</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor etchants market is divided into two main types: wet etching agents and dry etching agents. Wet etching agents utilize liquid solutions to remove material from semiconductor surfaces, offering excellent isotropy and control over etch rates for various materials. In contrast, dry etching agents employ gas-phase processes, such as plasma etching, providing high precision and anisotropic etching, ideal for complex geometries. Both methods are critical in semiconductor manufacturing, enabling the creation of intricate electronic devices through precise material removal.</p></p>
