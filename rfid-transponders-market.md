<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The RFID Transponders Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive RFID Transponders</li><li>Active RFID Transponders</li><li>Semi-Active RFID Transponders</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The RFID transponders market is divided into three main types: Passive RFID transponders, which rely on an external reader for power and have a limited range; Active RFID transponders, equipped with their own power source, allowing for longer read distances and more data storage; and Semi-Active RFID transponders, which combine features of both, using a battery for enhanced range while still relying on a reader for communication. Each type serves different applications based on range, cost, and data requirements.</p></p>
