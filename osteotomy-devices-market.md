<p><ul><li>Titanium Osteotomy Plates</li><li>Stainless Steel Osteotomy Plates</li><li>Polymer Osteotomy Plates</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The osteotomy devices market is categorized into three primary types: titanium osteotomy plates, stainless steel osteotomy plates, and polymer osteotomy plates. Titanium plates are lightweight, corrosion-resistant, and offer excellent compatibility with bone, making them popular for various orthopedic procedures. Stainless steel plates are strong and cost-effective but may be less biocompatible. Polymer plates, being flexible and increasingly used, provide a lightweight alternative with good biocompatibility, suitable for specific applications. Each type caters to diverse surgical requirements and patient needs.</p></p>
