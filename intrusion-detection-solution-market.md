It focuses on analyzing network traffic to identify and respond to security threats.</p><p>Host-based Intrusion Detection Solution (HIDS), on the other hand, monitors activities on individual devices such as servers or endpoints, looking for abnormal behavior that may indicate a security breach. It focuses on protecting specific devices or hosts within a network from potential threats.</p></p>
