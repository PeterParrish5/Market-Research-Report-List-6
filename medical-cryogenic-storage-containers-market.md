Biomedical freezers function at -10℃ to -50℃ for biological samples. Ultra-low freezers provide extreme cooling from -50℃ to -150℃ for sensitive items. Liquid nitrogen tanks operate at -150℃ to -196℃, ideal for long-term cryogenic preservation of cells and tissues.</p></p>
