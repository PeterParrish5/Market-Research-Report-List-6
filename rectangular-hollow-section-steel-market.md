<p><strong>The Rectangular Hollow Section Steel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>WeldedRHS</li><li>SeamlessRHS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rectangular Hollow Section Steel (RHS) is available in two main types: Welded RHS and Seamless RHS. Welded RHS is made by welding together steel sheets to form the rectangular shape, while Seamless RHS is created by extruding a single piece of steel to form the hollow section. Welded RHS is more commonly used due to its cost-effectiveness and availability in a wide range of sizes, while Seamless RHS is preferred for applications requiring higher strength and a smoother surface finish.</p></p>
