<p><p>Waterproof fabrics are divided into two main types: breathable and non-breathable. Breathable waterproof fabrics allow for moisture vapor to escape while preventing water from entering, making them ideal for outdoor activities. Non-breathable waterproof fabrics, on the other hand, do not allow for moisture vapor to escape, providing complete protection against water. Both types have their own advantages and are used in various applications based on the level of breathability and waterproofing required.</p></p>
