<p><p>Fiber optics cable assemblies are available in two main types: single mode and multiple mode. Single mode fibers have a smaller core size and transmit infrared laser light, making them ideal for long-distance communication. Multiple mode fibers have a larger core size and transmit multiple light modes simultaneously, making them suitable for shorter distances and lower bandwidth applications. Both types offer high-speed data transmission and are widely used in telecommunications, networking, and data centers.</p></p>
