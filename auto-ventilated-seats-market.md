<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Auto Ventilated Seats Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Radial Fans Seats</li><li>Axial Fans Seats</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The auto ventilated seats market is primarily categorized into two types: radial fan seats and axial fan seats. Radial fan seats utilize centrifugal fans to push air through the seat cushions, providing effective air circulation and cooling. In contrast, axial fan seats employ fans that draw air in parallel to the seating surface, promoting airflow directly against the occupant. Both types enhance comfort by regulating temperature and moisture, contributing to a more pleasant driving experience, especially in warm climates.</p></p>
