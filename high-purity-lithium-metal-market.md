<p><p>The high-purity lithium metal market primarily consists of two types: salt lake brine and lithium ore. Salt lake brine involves extracting lithium from salty underground reservoirs, which is often more cost-effective and environmentally friendly. In contrast, lithium ore extraction involves mining hard rock, typically spodumene, requiring more extensive processing to obtain lithium. Both sources are essential for producing high-purity lithium, which is crucial for applications in batteries, electric vehicles, and various advanced technologies, supporting the growing demand for sustainable energy solutions.</p></p>
