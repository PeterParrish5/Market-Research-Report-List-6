<p><p>The farmland irrigation valve market is primarily divided into two types: metal and plastic valves. Metal valves, typically made from materials like brass or stainless steel, offer durability and resistance to corrosion, making them suitable for high-pressure applications and longer lifespans. In contrast, plastic valves, often made from PVC or polyethylene, are lightweight, cost-effective, and resistant to chemicals, making them ideal for various irrigation systems. The choice between these types often depends on the specific requirements of the farming operation.</p></p>
