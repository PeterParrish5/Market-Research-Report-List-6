<p><strong>The Emergency Lighting System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Self-Contained Power System</li><li>Central Power System</li><li>Hybrid Power System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The emergency lighting system market is categorized into three main types: </p><p>1. **Self-Contained Power System**: This system includes battery-powered units that operate independently, providing immediate illumination during a power outage. </p><p>2. **Central Power System**: This system involves centralized battery backup, supplying power to multiple lighting fixtures from a single source, ensuring consistent performance across larger areas. </p><p>3. **Hybrid Power System**: This combines both self-contained and central power systems, offering flexibility and redundancy, enhancing reliability during emergencies.</p></p>
