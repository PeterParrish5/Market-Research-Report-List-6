<p><p>SBS, or Styrene Butadiene Styrene, can be divided into two main market types: oil-extended SBS and non-oil-extended SBS. Oil-extended SBS is a type of SBS that has been combined with various types of mineral oils to improve its flexibility and performance in applications such as asphalt modification and polymer modification. On the other hand, non-oil-extended SBS does not contain any added oils and is often preferred for applications where oil compatibility is a concern, such as in roofing and waterproofing materials.</p></p>
