<p><p>Autoradiography films are used in various fields such as medical diagnostics, scientific research, and industrial applications. The three main types of autoradiography films are Nuclear Emulsion, X-ray Film, and Others. Nuclear Emulsion films are sensitive to the emission of alpha, beta, and gamma radiation, making them ideal for high-resolution imaging in electron microscopy. X-ray films are commonly used for analyzing structures in biological samples. Other types of autoradiography films include phosphorimaging plates and photographic emulsions, each with their specific applications in radiography and imaging techniques.</p></p>
