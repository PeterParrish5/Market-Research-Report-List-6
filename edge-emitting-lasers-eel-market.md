<p><strong>The Edge Emitting Lasers (EEL) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Distributed Feedback Laser</li><li>Distributed Bragg Reflector Laser</li><li>Fabry-perot Laser</li><li>Broad Area Laser Diodes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Edge Emitting Lasers (EEL) are categorized into different types based on their designs and structures. Distributed Feedback Lasers have a grating structure that provides feedback for lasing. Distributed Bragg Reflector Lasers have mirrors built into the structure for reflection. Fabry-Perot Lasers use a resonator cavity for lasing. Broad Area Laser Diodes have a wider emitting area for higher power output. Each type of EEL has its own advantages and applications, making them suitable for various industrial and commercial uses.</p></p>
