<p><p>Pressure sandblasting machines are categorized into two main types: manual sandblasting machines and automatic sandblasting machines. Manual sandblasting machines require an operator to physically control the blasting process, offering more flexibility and customization but requiring more labor. On the other hand, automatic sandblasting machines operate without direct human intervention, offering higher efficiency and consistency in the blasting process. Both types cater to different market needs and preferences, with manual machines being more common in smaller operations and automatic machines in larger industrial settings.</p></p>
