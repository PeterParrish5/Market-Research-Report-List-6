The movement of the diaphragm creates suction and discharge pressure, allowing the pump to transfer liquids, slurries, or gases. <p><ul><li>Air Operated Diaphragm Pumps (AODD)</li><li>Electric Driven Diaphragm Pumps (EODD)</li><li>Piston Diaphragm Pumps</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Diaphragm pumps are available in different types based on their operation mechanism. Air Operated Diaphragm Pumps (AODD) are powered by compressed air, making them suitable for hazardous environments. Electric Driven Diaphragm Pumps (EODD) are powered by electricity, providing a continuous and reliable flow of liquid. Piston Diaphragm Pumps use a reciprocating piston to create the pumping action, offering high pressure capabilities. Each type of diaphragm pump caters to specific needs and applications in various industries.</p></p>
