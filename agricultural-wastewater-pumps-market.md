<p><strong>The Agricultural Wastewater Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-submersible</li><li>Submersible</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The agricultural wastewater pumps market is categorized into two main types: non-submersible and submersible pumps. Non-submersible pumps are installed above ground and are primarily used for draining water from basements or irrigation systems. They rely on suction to draw water. In contrast, submersible pumps are designed to operate underwater, making them efficient for directly pumping wastewater from wells, pits, or tanks. Both types serve vital roles in managing agricultural wastewater and enhancing irrigation practices.</p></p>
