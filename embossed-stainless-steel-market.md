<p><p>The embossed stainless steel market is categorized into three main types: 200 Series, 300 Series, and 400 Series, each defined by their composition and applications. The 200 Series, primarily using manganese and nitrogen, is cost-effective and corrosion-resistant, suitable for decorative and structural uses. The 300 Series, known for high nickel content, offers excellent corrosion resistance and is widely used in food processing and medical equipment. The 400 Series, which includes martensitic stainless steels, is magnetic and is used for automotive and kitchen applications due to its durability.</p></p>
