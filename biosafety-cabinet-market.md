<p><strong>The Biosafety Cabinet Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Class II A Type</li><li>Class II B Type</li><li>Class III Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Class II A Type biosafety cabinets are used for handling low to moderate risk biological agents. They provide protection to the environment and the user through HEPA-filtered air. Class II B Type cabinets offer a higher level of protection compared to Class II A, as they have a greater inflow velocity and double-ducted exhaust system. Class III Type cabinets are the most secure, providing the highest level of protection by completely enclosing the work area and using negative pressure within the cabinet.</p></p>
