<p><ul><li>Quartz Mask Plate</li><li>Soda Mask Version</li><li>Letterpress Mask Plate</li><li>Phenanthrene Mask Version</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor photomask market comprises various types, including quartz mask plates, soda mask versions, letterpress mask plates, and phenanthrene mask versions. Quartz mask plates are used for high-resolution applications due to their superior light transmission and durability. Soda mask versions, typically made from glass, are cost-effective for lower precision tasks. Letterpress mask plates allow precise pattern transfer for specific printing applications, while phenanthrene mask versions utilize organic materials to create tailored features. Each type serves distinct requirements in semiconductor fabrication processes.</p></p>
