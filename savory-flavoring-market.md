<p><p>Savory flavorings can be categorized into two main types: natural and synthetic. Natural flavorings are derived from sources found in nature, such as herbs, spices, and vegetables. They are typically extracted through processes like distillation or fermentation. On the other hand, synthetic flavorings are chemically created to mimic the taste of natural ingredients. While natural flavorings are seen as more authentic, synthetic flavorings offer consistency and cost-effectiveness. Both types play a significant role in the savory flavoring market, catering to different consumer preferences and needs.</p></p>
