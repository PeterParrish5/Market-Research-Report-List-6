<p><p>Digital Business Support Systems are typically offered in two market types: On-premise and Cloud-based. On-premise systems require hardware and software to be installed locally on a company's servers, providing more control and customization but also higher maintenance costs. On the other hand, Cloud-based systems are hosted on remote servers and accessed over the internet, offering lower upfront costs, scalability, and easier maintenance.
