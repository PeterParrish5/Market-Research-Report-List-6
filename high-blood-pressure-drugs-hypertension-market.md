<p><p>High Blood Pressure Drugs (Hypertension) Market is segmented into types such as ACEI (Angiotensin-Converting Enzyme Inhibitors), CCB (Calcium Channel Blockers), and ARB (Angiotensin II Receptor Blockers). ACEI drugs work by relaxing blood vessels, CCB drugs block calcium from entering the muscle cells of the heart and blood vessels, and ARB drugs block the action of a hormone that narrows blood vessels. These drugs are commonly used to treat high blood pressure and help lower the risk of heart attacks, strokes, and kidney problems.</p></p>
