<p><p>The miniature torque sensors market is divided into two main types: static torque sensors and dynamic torque sensors. Static torque sensors measure torque in stationary applications, providing precise feedback for calibration and testing in various industries. In contrast, dynamic torque sensors are designed for real-time torque measurement during motion, crucial for assessing performance in motors and rotating machinery. Both types are essential for improving system efficiency, ensuring reliability, and enhancing safety in applications across automotive, aerospace, and industrial automation sectors.</p></p>
