<p><p>The Airborne SATCOM Terminals market is primarily categorized into two types: Ka-band and Ku-band. Ka-band systems offer higher data rates and enhanced bandwidth, making them suitable for applications requiring high-speed internet and video communications. They operate at frequencies between 26.5 to 40 GHz. In contrast, Ku-band terminals, operating between 12 to 18 GHz, provide reliable communication with moderate data rates and wider coverage, making them popular for various aviation and defense applications. Each type serves distinct operational needs.</p></p>
