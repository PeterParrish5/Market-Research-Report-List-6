<p><p>The hydrogen market consists of two primary types: captive hydrogen and merchant hydrogen. Captive hydrogen is produced primarily for internal use within a facility or organization, often for specific processes like refining or manufacturing, ensuring that production meets the unique needs of the business. Conversely, the merchant hydrogen market involves hydrogen produced for sale to external customers, facilitating broader distribution and use across various industries, such as transportation, energy, and chemicals, while supporting market competition and pricing flexibility.</p></p>
