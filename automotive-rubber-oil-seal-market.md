EPDM oil seals are known for their excellent resistance to heat, UV exposure, and weathering, making them suitable for outdoor applications. NBR oil seals offer strong oil and fuel resistance, ideal for engine applications. SBR oil seals provide good abrasion resistance but are less effective in extreme temperatures. Other oil seals may include specialized materials for unique applications, catering to diverse automotive needs.</p></p>
