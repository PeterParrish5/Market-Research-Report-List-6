<p><p>A Coronary Dilatation Catheter is a specialized medical device used in the treatment of coronary artery diseases. It is primarily employed during percutaneous coronary interventions (PCIs) to dilate narrowed or obstructed arteries, thereby improving blood flow to the heart muscle. These catheters are equipped with a balloon that expands upon inflation, effectively widening the affected artery.</p><p>The Coronary Dilatation Catheter Market is witnessing substantial growth, driven by the rising prevalence of cardiovascular diseases, an aging population, and advancements in catheter technology.
