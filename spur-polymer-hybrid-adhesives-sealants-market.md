<p><p>SPUR polymer hybrid adhesives and sealants are categorized into one-component and two-component types. One-component systems are ready to use and generally cure upon exposure to moisture or specific conditions, making them convenient for quick applications. In contrast, two-component systems require a mixing process of an adhesive and a hardener, which activates the curing process, providing enhanced performance and durability for demanding environments. Both types serve diverse applications across industries, offering strong bonding and sealing capabilities.</p></p>
