<p><strong>The Wood Chippers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Self-Propelled Wood Chipper</li><li>PTO Wood Chipper</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wood chipper market is primarily segmented into self-propelled wood chippers and PTO wood chippers. Self-propelled wood chippers are equipped with their own engines, allowing for greater mobility and versatility, making them ideal for larger pruning or land-clearing jobs. In contrast, PTO (Power Take-Off) wood chippers are designed to be attached to tractors, relying on the tractor’s power for operation. They are often utilized for heavy-duty tasks, appealing to commercial users and large-scale operations requiring efficient wood processing.</p></p>
