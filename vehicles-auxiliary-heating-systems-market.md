<p><p>The vehicles auxiliary heating systems market primarily includes two types: fuel-based auxiliary heaters and electrical/Positive Temperature Coefficient (PTC) auxiliary heaters. Fuel-based heaters use combustion of fuel to generate heat, providing efficient warmth in colder climates without significantly draining vehicle batteries. In contrast, electrical or PTC heaters rely on electrical power to produce heat, offering a quicker response time and reduced emissions. Both types enhance passenger comfort and extend vehicle usability in low-temperature conditions, catering to diverse consumer preferences and requirements.</p></p>
