<p><p>NDT Pipeline Inspection Market Types encompass various methods such as Automated Ultrasonic Testing, Magnetic Particle Testing, Radiographic Testing, Penetrant Testing, and others. Automated Ultrasonic Testing utilizes automated systems to detect flaws and defects in pipelines. Magnetic Particle Testing involves the application of magnetic fields to identify surface and near-surface defects. Radiographic Testing uses X-rays or gamma rays to assess internal pipe quality. Penetrant Testing involves the application of penetrants to detect surface-breaking defects. Other methods include eddy current testing, visual testing, and acoustic emissions testing.</p></p>
