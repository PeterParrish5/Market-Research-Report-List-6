<p><ul><li>Chemical</li><li>Mineral</li><li>Fiber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The construction additives market comprises three main types: chemical, mineral, and fiber additives. Chemical additives enhance specific properties of construction materials, improving workability, strength, and durability. Mineral additives, such as fly ash or silica fume, are used to modify the physical characteristics of concrete and improve overall performance. Fiber additives, including steel, glass, and synthetic fibers, reinforce construction materials, enhancing tensile strength and reducing cracking. Together, these additives optimize construction processes and extend the lifespan of structures.</p></p>
