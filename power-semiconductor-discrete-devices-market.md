<p><p>Power semiconductor discrete devices market types include diodes, IGBTs (Insulated Gate Bipolar Transistors), MOSFETs (Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor Field-Effect Transistors), bipolar transistors (BJTs), and thyristors. Diodes allow current to flow in one direction, IGBTs combine the high input impedance of MOSFETs with the low on-state power loss of bipolar transistors, MOSFETs are voltage-controlled switches, BJTs are current-controlled switches, and thyristors are used for high power applications and require a trigger to turn on and off. These devices serve various applications in power electronics.</p></p>
