<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Strain Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Surface Strain Sensor</li><li>Transverse Strain Sensor</li><li>Longitudinal Strain Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The strain sensor market is segmented into three main types: surface strain sensors, transverse strain sensors, and longitudinal strain sensors. Surface strain sensors measure the deformation of surfaces under stress, making them ideal for applications like structural health monitoring. Transverse strain sensors gauge changes in dimensions perpendicular to the applied force, useful in material testing. Longitudinal strain sensors track elongation or compression along the direction of force, commonly employed in civil engineering and automotive sectors. Each type serves distinct monitoring and analysis needs.</p></p>
