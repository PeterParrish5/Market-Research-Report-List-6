<p><p>The red vacuum blood collection tube market is segmented into Common Serum Tubes and Rapid Serum Tubes. Common Serum Tubes are used for routine blood tests, effectively separating serum from blood cells after centrifugation. They typically contain no additives. Rapid Serum Tubes, on the other hand, expedite serum separation and are designed for quicker test results, often incorporating specific additives to enhance clotting. This segmentation caters to diverse laboratory needs, balancing efficiency and accuracy in diagnostics.</p></p>
