<p><p>Industrial I/O modules are essential components in automation systems for communication between field devices and control systems. PLC I/O modules are used in Programmable Logic Controllers to input and output signals for controlling various industrial processes. DCS I/O modules are designed specifically for Distributed Control Systems, providing data acquisition and control functions in a plant-wide network. PC-based I/O modules connect sensors and actuators directly to a computer for data acquisition and processing, offering flexibility and customization in industrial applications.</p></p>
