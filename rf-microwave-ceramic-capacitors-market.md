<p><p>RF & Microwave Ceramic Capacitors are categorized into two main types: Class 1 and Class 2. Class 1 Ceramic Capacitors are known for their high stability, low losses, and tight capacitance tolerances, making them ideal for critical timing and tuning applications in RF and microwave circuits. On the other hand, Class 2 Ceramic Capacitors offer higher capacitance values with lower stability and higher losses, making them suitable for decoupling, filtering, and bypass applications in RF and microwave circuits. Both types play crucial roles in ensuring reliable performance and efficiency in electronic systems.</p></p>
