<p><ul><li>Single-Layer (PP/PE)</li><li>Bilayer (PP/PE, PP/PP)</li><li>Trilayer (PP/PE/PP)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The lithium-ion battery separator market consists of three main types: single-layer, bilayer, and trilayer separators. Single-layer separators, typically made from polypropylene (PP) or polyethylene (PE), offer basic insulation and electrolyte retention. Bilayer separators combine materials like PP and PE or two PP layers for enhanced thermal stability and mechanical strength. Trilayer separators, featuring a configuration of PP/PE/PP, provide superior safety and improved ionic conductivity, making them ideal for high-performance battery applications where efficiency and reliability are critical.</p></p>
