<p><p>EVOH liners are commonly used in packaging to provide barrier protection against oxygen and other gases. There are three main types of EVOH liners based on their sealing method: stitched types, glued types, and other market. Stitched types are sealed using heat or ultrasonic welding, while glued types are sealed using adhesive. The "other market" category includes liners sealed using alternative methods such as pressure-sensitive adhesives or interlocking mechanisms. Each type offers unique benefits and applications depending on the specific packaging requirements.</p></p>
