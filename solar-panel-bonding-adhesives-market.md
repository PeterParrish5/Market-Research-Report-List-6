<p><strong>The Solar Panel Bonding Adhesives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Epoxy</li><li>Polyurethane</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The solar panel bonding adhesives market primarily comprises two types: epoxy and polyurethane. Epoxy adhesives offer high strength, excellent weather resistance, and thermal stability, making them ideal for long-lasting solar panel installations. They cure quickly and provide a rigid bond under various environmental conditions. On the other hand, polyurethane adhesives are more flexible, accommodating thermal expansion and contraction, which enhances durability. Their versatility and strong adhesion also make them suitable for various substrates, positioning them as essential components in solar panel assembly.</p></p>
