<p><p>The silicone elastomers market comprises three main types: High Temperature Vulcanised (HTV) silicone, which is robust and suitable for extreme temperatures; Room Temperature Vulcanised (RTV) silicone, which cures at ambient temperatures, making it versatile for various applications; and Liquid Silicone Rubber (LSR), characterized by its low viscosity and ease of processing, often used in precise manufacturing. Each type offers unique properties and benefits, catering to diverse industrial needs, including automotive, healthcare, electronics, and consumer goods.</p></p>
