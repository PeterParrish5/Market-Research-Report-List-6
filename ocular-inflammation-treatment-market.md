<p><p>The ocular inflammation treatment market encompasses various therapeutic types. Immunosuppressants reduce immune responses to alleviate inflammation, while monoclonal antibodies target specific inflammatory pathways. Corticosteroids provide rapid relief by dampening immune activity, and cycloplegic agents help relieve pain and photophobia by paralyzing the iris. Antibiotics manage secondary infections, analgesics alleviate pain, antivirals combat viral-induced inflammation, and antifungals address fungal infections. Together, these treatments address diverse causes and symptoms of ocular inflammation, enhancing patient outcomes.</p></p>
