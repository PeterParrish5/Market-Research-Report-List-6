<p><p>The solvent polyurethane adhesive market is categorized into three types: polyether, polyester, and mixed types. Polyether type adhesives offer excellent flexibility and resistance to moisture, making them ideal for applications in construction and automotive sectors. Polyester type adhesives provide superior strength and durability, suitable for high-stress environments. Mixed type adhesives combine the properties of both polyether and polyester, offering versatility for various applications. Each type addresses specific performance needs, enhancing adhesion in diverse industrial and commercial uses.</p></p>
