<p><ul><li>Crystalline Waterproofing Admixture</li><li>Pore-blocking Waterproofing Admixture</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The concrete waterproofing admixture market includes three primary types: crystalline waterproofing admixture, pore-blocking waterproofing admixture, and other alternatives. Crystalline waterproofing admixtures work by forming crystals within the concrete that block water pathways, enhancing durability. Pore-blocking admixtures seal tiny pores to prevent water ingress, improving surface impermeability. "Others" encompasses various innovative solutions, such as hydrophobic and flexible admixtures that cater to specific applications. Together, these types enhance concrete longevity and performance in moisture-prone environments.</p></p>
