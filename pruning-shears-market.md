<p><p>Pruning shears are available in three main types - pneumatic, electric, and manual. Pneumatic pruning shears are powered by compressed air and are suitable for heavy-duty pruning tasks. Electric pruning shears are powered by electricity and are ideal for larger branches and continuous use. Manual pruning shears are operated by hand and are best for smaller branches and precise trimming. Each type of pruning shears offers unique advantages and is chosen based on the specific pruning needs and preferences of the user.</p></p>
