<p><p>The miniature force sensors market includes various technologies. Piezoresistive force sensors utilize changes in resistance to measure force, offering high sensitivity. Ultrasonic force sensors use sound waves to detect variations in force, providing non-contact measurement. Capacitive force sensors measure changes in capacitance due to force application, enabling precise detection. Optical force sensors use light to gauge force levels, offering high accuracy. Magnetic force sensors detect force through magnetic field variations, while "other" sensors encompass emerging technologies and specialized applications.</p></p>
