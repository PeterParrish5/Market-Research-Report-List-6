<p><p>Counting scales can be categorized into two primary market types based on their power source: rechargeable battery and AC power. Rechargeable battery-operated counting scales offer the advantage of portability, making them ideal for use in locations without direct access to electricity. In contrast, AC power scales provide continuous operation and are typically used in fixed positions, such as manufacturing plants or warehouses, where they benefit from a consistent power supply. Both types serve essential roles in inventory management and logistics.</p></p>
