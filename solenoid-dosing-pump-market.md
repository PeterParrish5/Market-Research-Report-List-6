<p><p>Solenoid dosing pumps are available in two main types: manual control and signal control. In manual control pumps, dosing rates and frequencies are manually set by the operator. These pumps are typically used in applications where dosing requirements are stable and do not change frequently. On the other hand, signal control pumps are equipped with sensors and controllers that automatically adjust dosing rates based on specific signals or input parameters. These pumps are suitable for applications where dosing requirements vary or need to be closely monitored and controlled.</p></p>
