<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Leaded ceramic frit is categorized into two primary market types: Type I and Type II. Type I frits contain higher lead oxide content, offering enhanced durability and improved gloss, making them ideal for decorative and high-performance applications. In contrast, Type II frits have lower lead content, focusing on safety and regulatory compliance while still providing decent adhesion and gloss. These distinctions help manufacturers select the appropriate frit type based on desired performance characteristics and environmental considerations in their products.</p></p>
