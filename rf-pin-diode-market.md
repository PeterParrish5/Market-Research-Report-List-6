<p><ul><li>Vertical PIN Diodes</li><li>Horizontal PIN Diodes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The RF PIN diode market consists of two primary types: vertical and horizontal PIN diodes. Vertical PIN diodes feature a structure that aligns the layers vertically, providing compactness and efficient heat dissipation, making them suitable for high-frequency applications. In contrast, horizontal PIN diodes have a flat design that supports better electrical performance and larger power handling, often preferred for broadband and high-power applications. Both types play crucial roles in RF signal switching, attenuation, and modulation in various telecommunications and electronics sectors.</p></p>
