<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The RAID Cards Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>RAID 0</li><li>RAID 1</li><li>RAID 2</li><li>RAID 3</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>RAID cards serve various data storage needs, categorized by RAID levels. RAID 0 offers striping for high performance but no redundancy. RAID 1 mirrors data across drives, ensuring redundancy but halving available storage. RAID 2 uses bit-level striping with Hamming code for error correction, while RAID 3 employs byte-level striping with a dedicated parity drive for fault tolerance. Other RAID configurations include RAID 4, RAID 5, and RAID 6, which balance performance, capacity, and data protection in diverse applications.</p></p>
