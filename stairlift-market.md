<p><p>Stairlift market can be broadly categorized into two types: Straight Stairlifts and Curved Stairlifts. Straight Stairlifts are designed for staircases that have a simple straight line, making them a more affordable and easier to install option. On the other hand, Curved Stairlifts are customized to fit staircases with curves or bends, offering a solution for more complex staircases. Both types cater to different needs and preferences, ensuring that individuals with mobility challenges have options that suit their specific requirements.</p></p>
