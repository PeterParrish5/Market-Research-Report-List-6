<p><strong>The Non-Woven Adhesive Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SBC-based Adhesives</li><li>APAO-based Adhesives</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The non-woven adhesive market is primarily segmented into SBC-based (Styrene-Butadiene Copolymer) adhesives and APAO-based (Amorphous Polyalphaolefin) adhesives. SBC-based adhesives offer excellent elasticity and adhesion properties, making them suitable for applications like diapers and feminine hygiene products. In contrast, APAO-based adhesives provide superior thermal stability and waterproof characteristics, ideal for applications in medical and industrial textiles. Both types are essential in various non-woven applications, catering to diverse consumer needs and enhancing product performance.</p></p>
