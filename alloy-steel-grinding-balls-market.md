<p><p>Alloy steel grinding balls are predominantly divided into two categories based on their production methods: forged steel grinding balls and cast steel grinding balls. Forged steel grinding balls are produced by heating steel bars and then forming them into spherical shapes through repeated impact. Cast steel grinding balls are created by pouring molten steel into molds to achieve the desired shape. Both types of grinding balls are widely used in industries such as mining and cement production for crushing and grinding materials.</p></p>
