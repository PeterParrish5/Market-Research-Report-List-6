<p><p>General Aviation Engines Market includes four main types of engines: </p><p>1. Turboprop Engine: These engines use a gas turbine to drive a propeller, providing efficient power for small to medium-sized aircraft.</p><p>2. Turbofan Engine: Known for their high bypass ratio, turbofan engines are commonly used in commercial airliners for their fuel efficiency and lower noise levels.</p><p>3. Turboshaft Engine: Typically used in helicopters, turboshaft engines convert power from the turbine into rotational force for the helicopter's rotor.</p><p>4. Piston Engine: Commonly found in small general aviation aircraft, piston engines use reciprocating motion to generate power.</p></p>
