<p><p>Edge controllers are devices used to manage and control edge computing applications. There are two main types of edge controllers based on their installation method: DIN-rail and panel mount. DIN-rail edge controllers are designed to be mounted on standard DIN rails commonly found in industrial control panels. Panel mount edge controllers are intended for mounting directly onto control panels or walls. Both types cater to different installation requirements and offer flexibility in deploying edge computing solutions in various industrial settings.</p></p>
