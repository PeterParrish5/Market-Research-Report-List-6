<p><ul><li>Manual Pressure Infusor</li><li>Automatic Pressure Infusor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Pressure Infusion Devices market comprises two main types: Manual Pressure Infusors and Automatic Pressure Infusors. Manual Pressure Infusors require healthcare professionals to exert pressure manually on a bag containing fluids, allowing controlled infusion during medical procedures. In contrast, Automatic Pressure Infusors utilize mechanized systems to maintain consistent pressure, reducing the risk of human error and ensuring more precise fluid delivery. Both types are essential in critical care, enhancing the effectiveness and efficiency of fluid administration in various medical settings.</p></p>
