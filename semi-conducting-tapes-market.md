<p><p>Semi conducting tapes are specialized adhesive products that exhibit electrical conductivity. They are primarily used in the electronics and electrical sectors for applications such as EMI/RFI shielding, grounding, and connection of components in circuit assemblies. These tapes can be made from various materials, including polymer films infused with conductive fillers, which enhance their electrical properties while maintaining adhesive functions.</p><p>The Semi Conducting Tapes Market is experiencing robust growth, driven by the increasing demand for advanced electronic devices, including smartphones, laptops, and automotive components.
