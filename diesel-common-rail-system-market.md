<p><ul><li>Solenoid Valve Type</li><li>Piezoelectric</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Diesel Common Rail System market is primarily segmented into two types: solenoid valve type and piezoelectric type. The solenoid valve type utilizes electromagnetic actuators to control fuel injection, offering a reliable and cost-effective solution for diesel engines. In contrast, the piezoelectric type employs piezoelectric materials that provide faster response times and more precise control of fuel injection, enhancing performance and efficiency. Both types cater to various diesel engine applications, contributing to advancements in fuel efficiency and emissions reduction.</p></p>
