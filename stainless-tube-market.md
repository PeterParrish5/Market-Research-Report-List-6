<p><p>The stainless tube market is primarily divided into two types: welded and seamless tubes. Welded tubes are formed by joining metal pieces together, making them suitable for various applications that require flexibility and cost-effectiveness. Seamless tubes, on the other hand, are manufactured without joints, providing superior strength and integrity, which is essential for high-pressure and high-temperature applications. Both types serve diverse industries, including construction, automotive, and aerospace, with specific advantages based on their composition and production methods.</p></p>
