Low voltage circuit breakers operate up to 1 kV, primarily protecting homes and small businesses from electrical faults. Medium voltage breakers function between 1 kV and 36 kV, commonly used in industrial applications and utility distribution systems. High voltage circuit breakers, operating above 36 kV, are essential for managing large-scale electrical grids, ensuring reliability and safety in power transmission.</p></p>
