<p><p>The aluminum fluoride (AlF3) market comprises three key types: dry AlF3, anhydrous AlF3, and wet AlF3. Dry AlF3 is often used in the aluminum smelting process, providing fluoride in a solid form. Anhydrous AlF3, being moisture-free, is utilized in high-performance applications like ceramics and glass manufacturing. Wet AlF3, which contains water, is typically employed in chemical processes and the production of aluminum metal. Each type serves distinct industrial applications based on its properties and moisture content.</p></p>
