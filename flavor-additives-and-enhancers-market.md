<p><p>The Flavor Additives and Enhancers market comprises two main types: flavor additives and flavor enhancers. Flavor additives are substances added to foods to provide specific tastes and aromas, enhancing overall sensory appeal. Common examples include natural and artificial flavorings. Flavor enhancers, on the other hand, do not have a distinct taste themselves but amplify existing flavors in food products, making them more palatable. Together, they play a vital role in food formulation across various industries, including snacks, beverages, and processed foods.</p></p>
