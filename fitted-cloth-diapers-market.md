<p><p>Fitted cloth diapers come in two main types: single-layer and multi-layer. Single-layer diapers consist of just one layer of absorbent material, making them lightweight and less bulky. On the other hand, multi-layer diapers have several layers of absorbent material sewn together, providing higher levels of absorbency and protection. Single-layer diapers are typically preferred for daytime use, while multi-layer diapers are commonly used for overnight or heavy wetters. Both types offer eco-friendly and cost-effective alternatives to disposable diapers.</p></p>
