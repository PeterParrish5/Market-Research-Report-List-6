<p><p>The Automated Passenger Counting (APC) System market encompasses various technologies for accurately tracking passenger numbers in public transport. The Infrared Type uses beams to detect entries and exits. Time-of-Flight Type measures the time taken for a light pulse to bounce back from passengers, ensuring precise counts. Stereoscopic Vision Type employs cameras and depth sensing to analyze passenger movement and volume, providing detailed insights. Each type serves to enhance operational efficiency and improve service delivery in transit systems.</p></p>
