<p><p>The geothermal heat pump system market encompasses three main types: </p><p>1. **Underground Tube Ground Source Heat Pump Systems** utilize buried loops of pipes filled with fluid to exchange heat with the ground, providing efficient heating and cooling. </p><p>2. **Groundwater Source Heat Pump Systems** draw heat directly from underground water sources, offering high efficiency but requiring access to groundwater. </p><p>3. **Surface Water Ground Source Heat Pump Systems** extract heat from nearby bodies of water, leveraging their stable temperature for energy-efficient climate control in buildings.</p></p>
