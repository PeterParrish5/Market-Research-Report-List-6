<p><p>Rutile is a mineral composed mainly of titanium dioxide. There are two main types of rutile markets - natural rutile and synthetic rutile. Natural rutile is obtained from mineral deposits, primarily in Australia and South Africa, and is known for its high purity and quality. Synthetic rutile, on the other hand, is produced through a chemical process using ilmenite ore and is typically lower in purity compared to natural rutile. Both types of rutile are commonly used in industries such as pigment production, ceramics, and welding electrodes.</p></p>
