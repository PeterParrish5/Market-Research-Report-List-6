<p><p>Medical waste management services encompass various treatment methods for hazardous healthcare waste, primarily including incineration, autoclaving, and chemical disinfection. Incineration involves burning waste at high temperatures, effectively reducing its volume and eliminating pathogens. Autoclaving uses steam under pressure to sterilize waste, making it safe for disposal. Chemical disinfection involves applying chemicals to decontaminate waste, rendering it non-hazardous. Each method caters to different waste types and regulatory requirements, ensuring safe and environmentally responsible waste management in healthcare settings.</p></p>
