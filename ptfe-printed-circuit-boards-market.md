<p><p>The PTFE Printed Circuit Boards (PCBs) market is characterized by two primary types: single-layer and multi-layer boards. Single-layer PCBs consist of a single layer of substrate material with conductive traces, making them simpler and cost-effective for basic applications. In contrast, multi-layer PCBs comprise multiple interconnected layers, allowing for more complex circuits and higher density. These boards are essential in advanced electronics, offering improved performance, thermal stability, and signal integrity, making them suitable for high-frequency and high-reliability applications.</p></p>
