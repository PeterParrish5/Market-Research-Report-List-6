<p><p>The CNC rebar bending machine market is divided into two primary types based on operation: manual and automatic. Manual operation machines require human intervention for bending tasks, offering simplicity and lower initial costs, making them suitable for smaller projects. In contrast, automatic operation machines utilize computer numerical control technology to perform precise and efficient bending with minimal human input. This enhances productivity, reduces errors, and is ideal for large-scale construction projects where speed and accuracy are essential.</p></p>
