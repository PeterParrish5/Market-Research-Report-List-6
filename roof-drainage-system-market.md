<p><strong>The Roof Drainage System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gravity Drainage System</li><li>Siphonic Drainage System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Roof Drainage System market includes two main types: Gravity Drainage System and Siphonic Drainage System. Gravity Drainage System relies on the force of gravity to move water from the roof to the ground, making it a traditional and cost-effective option. On the other hand, Siphonic Drainage System uses a vacuum effect to rapidly remove water from the roof, allowing for larger volumes of water to be moved efficiently. Both systems play a crucial role in managing water runoff and preventing damage to buildings.</p></p>
