CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) sensors are known for their high-quality image capture and low noise levels, making them ideal for applications requiring high image clarity. On the other hand, CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) sensors are known for their lower power consumption and faster performance, making them suitable for applications requiring fast image processing. Both CCD and CMOS sensors play crucial roles in the Automotive Mono Camera market, catering to different requirements and applications.</p></p>
