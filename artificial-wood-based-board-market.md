<p><p>The artificial wood-based board market encompasses several types, including particle board, plywood, oriented strand board (OSB), medium-density fiberboard (MDF), high-density fiberboard (HDF), and hardboard. Particle board is made from wood particles and adhesives, while plywood consists of thin layers of wood veneer glued together. OSB features strands and is engineered for strength. MDF and HDF are created from wood fibers under heat and pressure, offering smooth finishes. Hardboard, made from compressed wood fibers, is denser and used for various applications, including furniture and cabinetry.</p></p>
