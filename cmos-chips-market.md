<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The CMOS Chips Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Pixel Type</li><li>Passive Pixel Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The CMOS chips market is primarily divided into two types: Active Pixel Sensors (APS) and Passive Pixel Sensors (PPS). Active Pixel Type chips integrate amplifying transistors at each pixel, enhancing image quality and allowing for faster readout, making them ideal for applications in digital cameras and smartphones. Conversely, Passive Pixel Type chips lack individual transistors, resulting in lower image quality and slower performance. These distinctions define their applications, with APS preferred for high-performance imaging and PPS used in lower-end devices.</p></p>
