<p><strong>The Electron-beam Writer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gaussian-beam EBL Systems</li><li>Shaped-beam EBL Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electron-beam Writer (EBL) market is primarily categorized into two types: Gaussian-beam EBL systems and Shaped-beam EBL systems. Gaussian-beam EBL systems utilize a finely focused electron beam that varies in intensity, allowing for high-resolution patterning, commonly used in semiconductor applications. In contrast, Shaped-beam EBL systems employ predefined shapes of the electron beam, enabling faster writing speeds and better throughput for larger areas. Both types serve critical roles in nanofabrication, impacting industries like electronics and materials science.</p></p>
