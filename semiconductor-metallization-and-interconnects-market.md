Flash Evaporation is a rapid heating process for evaporation. Induction Evaporation uses electromagnetic induction to evaporate metals. Sputtering involves bombarding a target material with high-energy particles to create a thin film. Others include various alternative methods for metallization and interconnects in semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
