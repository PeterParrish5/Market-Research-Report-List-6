<p><p>Mercury analyzers are devices used to measure the presence of mercury in various samples. There are two main types of mercury analyzers: Cold Vapor Atomic Absorption Mercury Analyzer and Cold Atomic Fluorescence Mercury Analyzer. The Cold Vapor Atomic Absorption Mercury Analyzer uses a technique that involves converting mercury into a vapor before analysis, while the Cold Atomic Fluorescence Mercury Analyzer uses atomic fluorescence to detect mercury at very low levels. Both analyzers are commonly used in environmental testing, industrial processes, and research laboratories.</p></p>
