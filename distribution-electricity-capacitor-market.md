<p><p>The Distribution Electricity Capacitor Market can be categorized into three main types: </p><p>1. **Shunt Capacitors** are used to improve power factor and reduce losses by providing reactive power locally to distribution systems. </p><p>2. **Series Capacitors** are employed to enhance voltage stability and increase power transmission capacity between connected networks. </p><p>3. **Circuit Breaker Capacitors** are integrated with circuit breakers to enhance their operational performance by improving arc extinction and voltage stability during fault conditions. Each type serves a unique role in optimizing electrical distribution systems.</p></p>
