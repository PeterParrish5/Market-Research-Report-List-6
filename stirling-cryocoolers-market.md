<p><p>Stirling Cryocoolers are commonly categorized into two main types: linear cryocoolers and rotary cryocoolers. Linear cryocoolers operate by moving a piston or displacer back and forth to compress and expand the working fluid, creating the necessary cooling effect. On the other hand, rotary cryocoolers use a rotating mechanism to achieve the same result. Both types of cryocoolers are widely used in various industries such as medical, military, and aerospace for their ability to provide reliable and efficient cooling solutions.</p></p>
