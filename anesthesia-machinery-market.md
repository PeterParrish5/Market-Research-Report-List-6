DC anesthesia machines rely on direct current for their operation, offering precision and efficiency in drug delivery. Closed anesthesia machines provide a controlled environment, recycling exhaled gases while minimizing waste, thus enhancing patient safety and reducing environmental impact. Each type serves distinct needs in medical practices.</p></p>
