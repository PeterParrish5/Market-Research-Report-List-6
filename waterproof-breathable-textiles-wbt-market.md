<p><p>Waterproof Breathable Textiles (WBT) are engineered fabrics that repel water while allowing vapor to escape, ensuring comfort in wet conditions. The market features two primary types: PTFE (Polytetrafluoroethylene) and EPFE (Expanded Polytetrafluoroethylene). PTFE is known for its durability and excellent waterproofing properties, making it ideal for outdoor gear. EPFE, on the other hand, offers enhanced breathability and flexibility, suitable for applications requiring increased comfort and movement. Both types cater to various industries, including outdoor apparel and industrial use.</p></p>
