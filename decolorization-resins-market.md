<p><strong>The Decolorization Resins Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Decolorization resins are categorized mainly into Type I and Type II. Type I resins, often used for treating highly acidic solutions, are effective in removing color and impurities from water and various liquids. They are strong base anion exchange resins, offering high selectivity. Type II resins, on the other hand, are suited for less acidic environments and provide enhanced removal of color and organic compounds. These resins find applications in industries such as food, pharmaceuticals, and water treatment.</p></p>
