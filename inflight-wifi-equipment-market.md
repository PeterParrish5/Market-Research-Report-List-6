<p><strong>The Inflight WIFI Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ATG</li><li>Ka Band Satellite</li><li>Ku Band Satellite</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Inflight WIFI equipment market consists of three main types: ATG (Air-to-Ground), Ka Band Satellite, and Ku Band Satellite. ATG systems use ground-based antennas to connect to the internet, offering relatively fast and cost-effective connectivity. Ka Band Satellite systems provide higher bandwidth and faster speeds, but are more expensive to install and operate. Ku Band Satellite systems offer slightly lower bandwidth than Ka Band, but are more cost-effective. Each type has its own advantages and limitations, catering to different needs and budgets in the aviation industry.</p></p>
