<p><p>The rice steamer market can be categorized into three main types: Conventional Rice Cookers, Micom Rice Cookers, and IH Rice Cookers. Conventional rice cookers are basic models that usually have a simple on/off function. Micom rice cookers utilize microcomputer technology to adjust cooking time and temperature for optimal results. IH rice cookers use induction heating to heat the entire inner cooking pan evenly, resulting in more precise and efficient cooking. Each type offers different features and capabilities to cater to varying consumer needs and preferences.</p></p>
