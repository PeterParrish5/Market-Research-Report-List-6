<p><p>The bench-top sterilizer market includes several types designed for different sterilization methods. Steam sterilizers use high-pressure steam to effectively eliminate microorganisms, making them ideal for heat-stable instruments. Plasma sterilizers utilize low-temperature hydrogen peroxide gas plasma, suitable for sensitive electronics and heat-sensitive items. Hot air sterilizers employ dry heat at elevated temperatures, often for metal instruments. Other methods may include chemical sterilizers using ethylene oxide or ozone, catering to specific needs in medical, laboratory, and research settings.</p></p>
