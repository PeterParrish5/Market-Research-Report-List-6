<p><p>The CABG (Coronary Artery Bypass Grafting) surgery device market includes various graft types used to restore blood flow to the heart. Saphenous vein grafts utilize veins from the leg for bypass, while internal thoracic artery grafts use arteries from the chest, known for superior long-term outcomes. Other devices may include off-pump systems, endoscopic tools, and anastomosis devices, which enhance surgical efficiency and patient recovery. Each type plays a crucial role in improving surgical techniques and patient results in coronary bypass surgery.</p></p>
