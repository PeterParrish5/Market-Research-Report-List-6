<p><ul><li>Non-Vented Drip Chambers</li><li>Vented Drip Chambers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Non-vented drip chambers are designed without vents to prevent air from entering the infusion line, ensuring accurate flow of fluids. These are commonly used in IV sets for precise medication administration. Vented drip chambers, on the other hand, have vents that allow air to enter the chamber, facilitating consistent flow rates and preventing air bubbles from obstructing the IV line. Both types are essential components in medical devices such as IV drips, ensuring safe and efficient delivery of fluids to patients.</p></p>
