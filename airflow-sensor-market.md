<p><ul><li>Vane Airflow Sensor</li><li>Karman Vortex Airflow Sensor</li><li>Hot Wire Airflow Sensor</li><li>Hot Film Airflow Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Airflow Sensor market comprises various types, each tailored for specific applications. Vane airflow sensors use rotating paddles to measure air velocity, making them suitable for HVAC systems. Karman vortex sensors detect flow rate by analyzing vortices created in the airflow, ideal for industrial applications. Hot wire airflow sensors measure temperature changes in a heated wire, providing high accuracy for low-speed flows, while hot film sensors utilize thin films to assess air velocity, often used in automotive and aerospace sectors.</p></p>
