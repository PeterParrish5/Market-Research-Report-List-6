<p><p>Business printer market types include lithographic, digital, flexographic, screen, and gravure print technologies. Lithographic printing is a traditional method that uses plates to transfer ink onto paper. Digital printing is more modern, using digital files to directly print onto paper. Flexographic printing is commonly used for packaging materials. Screen printing involves ink being pressed through a mesh screen onto the paper. Gravure printing uses engraved cylinders to transfer ink. Other market types may include label printing, textile printing, and 3D printing.</p></p>
