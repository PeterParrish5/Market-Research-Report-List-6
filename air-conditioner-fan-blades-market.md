<p><p>Air conditioner fan blades market consists of two main types: cross flow fan blades and centrifugal fan blades. </p><p>Cross flow fan blades are commonly used in smaller air conditioning units and have a cylindrical shape with blades that extend outward from the center. They are ideal for cooling smaller spaces efficiently.</p><p>Centrifugal fan blades, on the other hand, are used in larger air conditioning units and have curved blades that create a centrifugal force to move air. They are suitable for cooling larger areas effectively.</p></p>
