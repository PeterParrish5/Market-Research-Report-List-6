<p><p>The hospital-acquired infection (HAI) testing market encompasses various testing methods, including molecular diagnostics, blood tests, and urinalysis. Molecular diagnostics enable rapid detection of pathogens using DNA or RNA analysis, facilitating timely treatment decisions. Blood tests identify infections through serological markers or culture methods, crucial for managing systemic infections. Urinalysis, focusing on urine samples, helps detect urinary tract infections, a common HAI. Together, these testing types enhance diagnosis efficiency, improve patient outcomes, and help manage infection control in healthcare settings.</p></p>
