<p><p>Regular (non-adhesive, non-printed) microscope slides are the traditional type of slides used for mounting and viewing specimens under a microscope. Adhesive microscope slides have a coating of adhesive to prevent the specimen from moving during observation. Pattern printed microscope slides have printed grids or patterns to assist in measuring and locating specific areas on the specimen. Other types of microscope slides include specialty slides with unique coatings or features tailored for specific applications, such as fluorescence microscopy or live-cell imaging.</p></p>
