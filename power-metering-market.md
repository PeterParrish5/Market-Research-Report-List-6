<p><p>The power metering market comprises three main types: Analog meters, Digital meters, and Smart meters. Analog meters are traditional devices with mechanical displays that measure electricity consumption using dials. Digital meters offer electronic readings and can provide more accurate consumption data. Smart meters enhance functionality by enabling two-way communication, allowing real-time data transmission, remote monitoring, and integration with smart grid technologies. This evolution reflects a shift toward better energy management, efficiency, and consumer engagement in energy consumption.</p></p>
