<p><p>Atomic Force Microscopy (AFM) is a powerful imaging technique that uses a cantilever with a sharp tip to explore the surface at the atomic level. The AFM market segments include Contact AFM, which applies a constant force between the tip and the surface; Non-contact AFM, where the tip interacts at a distance; Dynamic Contact AFM, which oscillates the cantilever to enhance imaging; and Tapping AFM, where the tip taps the surface intermittently, minimizing damage while providing high-resolution images.</p></p>
