<p><ul><li>Tissue Fixation</li><li>Bone Fixation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>ACL reconstruction market consists of two major types of fixation methods: tissue fixation and bone fixation. Tissue fixation involves securing the new ligament graft with special screws or anchors to the surrounding soft tissue. On the other hand, bone fixation involves attaching the graft to the bone using screws or tunnels drilled into the bone. Both methods have their own advantages and disadvantages, with tissue fixation offering less stress on the graft and bone fixation providing better initial stability.</p></p>
