<p><p>The master cylinder actuation market encompasses three primary types: hydraulic, air, and mechanical. Hydraulic master cylinders utilize fluid pressure to transmit force for braking or clutch operations, providing efficient and responsive performance. Air-type actuations leverage compressed air, often seen in commercial vehicles, offering lightweight and quicker actuation. Mechanical types rely on direct physical connection using levers or cables, typically found in older systems or specific designs. Each type serves distinct applications, contributing to various automotive and industrial needs.</p></p>
