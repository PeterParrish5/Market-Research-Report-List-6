<p><p>Mammography imaging market can be divided into two types: analog and digital. Analog mammography uses X-ray film to capture breast images, while digital mammography uses electronic detectors to convert X-rays into digital images. Digital mammography offers advantages such as faster image processing, easier storage and sharing of images, and improved image quality. Despite these advantages, analog mammography systems are still used in some healthcare facilities due to lower cost and familiarity. Both types of mammography play a crucial role in the early detection of breast cancer.</p></p>
