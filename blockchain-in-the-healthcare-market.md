<p><p>Blockchain technology in the healthcare market can be categorized into three main types: public, private, and permissioned blockchains. Public blockchains are decentralized and open to anyone to participate and view the data. Private blockchains restrict access to authorized users only and are commonly used within organizations. Permissioned blockchains combine aspects of both public and private blockchains, allowing for controlled access to specific participants. Each type offers unique benefits and security features tailored to the specific needs of the healthcare industry.</p></p>
