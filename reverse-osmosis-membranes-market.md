<p><p>Reverse osmosis membranes are vital components in water purification systems. The three main types of reverse osmosis membranes are Cellulose Acetate Membrane, Aromatic Hydrazide Membrane, and Aromatic Polyamide Membrane. </p><p>Cellulose Acetate Membrane is known for its high water permeability, making it suitable for low-pressure applications. Aromatic Hydrazide Membrane offers excellent salt rejection properties, making it ideal for desalination processes. Aromatic Polyamide Membrane is the most commonly used type due to its high efficiency in removing contaminants from water.</p></p>
