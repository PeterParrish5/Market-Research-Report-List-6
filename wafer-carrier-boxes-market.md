<p><p>The Wafer Carrier Boxes market includes two primary types: Front Opening Shipping Box (FOSB) and Front Opening Unified Pod (FOUP). FOSB is designed for transporting wafers between fabrication facilities while maintaining cleanliness and protecting against physical damage. In contrast, FOUPs are specifically engineered for automated handling in semiconductor manufacturing, offering enhanced protection and contamination control during processing. Both types are crucial for the semiconductor industry, ensuring the safe and efficient transport of sensitive silicon wafers.</p></p>
