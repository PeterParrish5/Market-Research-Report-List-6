<p><p>Mineral wool material is available in various market types catering to different needs. Felty mineral wool is produced in felt form which provides flexibility and ease of use. Strip mineral wool is manufactured in long strips, making it ideal for insulation purposes. Tubular mineral wool is shaped in cylindrical tubes, suitable for piping insulation. Granular mineral wool is produced in small granules, ideal for filling cavities. Plate mineral wool comes in flat plates, perfect for wall and ceiling insulation. Each type serves a specific purpose in the construction industry.</p></p>
