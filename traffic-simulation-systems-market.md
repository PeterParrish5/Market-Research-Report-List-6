<p><p>Traffic simulation systems can be categorized into three main types: </p><p>1. **Microscopic Simulation** focuses on individual vehicle movements and interactions, providing detailed analysis of traffic flow at a granular level.</p><p>2. **Mesoscopic Simulation** balances detail and computational efficiency, modeling groups of vehicles and their behavior within specific regions.</p><p>3. **Macroscopic Simulation** uses aggregated data to analyze traffic flow patterns and system performance across larger areas.</p><p>Traffic planning simulations integrate these types to optimize infrastructure development and traffic management strategies.</p></p>
