<p><p>Molecular Beacon Probes are classified into two main types: Tag Molecular Beacon Probes and Label-free Molecular Beacon Probes. Tag Molecular Beacon Probes use a fluorescent or chemiluminescent dye to detect the target nucleic acid sequence, while Label-free Molecular Beacon Probes rely on a stem-loop structure to generate a signal upon binding to the target sequence. Both types offer advantages in terms of sensitivity, specificity, and ease of use, making them valuable tools in various research and diagnostic applications.</p></p>
