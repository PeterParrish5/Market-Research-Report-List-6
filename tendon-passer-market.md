<p><p>Tendon passers are surgical instruments used to facilitate the passage of artificial tendons through bone tunnels during orthopedic procedures. They are available in two main market types: straight and curved. Straight tendon passers are designed for linear bone tunnel trajectories, while curved tendon passers are used for more complex or angled tunnel paths. Surgeons may choose between these market types based on the specific anatomical considerations of the patient and the requirements of the surgical procedure.</p></p>
