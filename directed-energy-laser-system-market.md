<p><p>Directed-energy laser systems are classified into various types based on the technology used. Gas lasers use gas mixtures as the lasing medium, while chemical lasers rely on a chemical reaction to generate a laser beam. Excimer lasers utilize reactive gases to produce short-wavelength UV light. Fiber lasers use optical fibers as the gain medium, enabling high power and efficiency. Other types include solid-state lasers, semiconductor lasers, and free-electron lasers. Each type offers unique benefits and applications in the directed-energy market.</p></p>
