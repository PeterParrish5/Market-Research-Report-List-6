<p><p>Glass sealants are used to provide protection to glass surfaces from moisture, dirt, and foreign particles. Single-component sealants come ready to use and do not require the mixing of additional components, making them easy to apply. Both types of sealants serve the same purpose of protecting glass surfaces, but differ in terms of application and performance.</p></p>
