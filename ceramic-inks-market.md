<p><p>Ceramic inks can be divided into two main types: functional inks and decorative inks. Functional inks are designed for specific purposes such as conductivity, resistance to abrasion, or anti-bacterial properties. These inks are typically used in applications where performance is critical, such as in the aerospace or electronics industries. On the other hand, decorative inks are used for aesthetic purposes, adding color and design to ceramic products like tiles, tableware, and architectural elements. Both types of inks play a crucial role in the ceramic industry by providing both functional benefits and visual appeal.</p></p>
