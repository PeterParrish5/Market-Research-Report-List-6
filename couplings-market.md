<p><p>Couplings are essential components in mechanical systems, categorized into four market types: Non-Shifting, Shifting, Hydrodynamic, and Magnetic. Non-Shifting couplings connect shafts without movement, ensuring a rigid connection. Shifting couplings allow for positional adjustment between shafts. Hydrodynamic couplings use fluid to transmit power, providing smooth engagement and shock absorption. Magnetic couplings employ magnetic forces for contactless transmission, reducing wear and enhancing efficiency. Each type serves distinct applications, ensuring optimal performance and reliability in various mechanical systems.</p></p>
