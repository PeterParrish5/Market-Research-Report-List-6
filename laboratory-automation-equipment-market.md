<p><strong>The Laboratory Automation Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Task Targeted Automation (TTA)</li><li>Laboratory Automation (TLA)</li><li>Separate Automation Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Laboratory Automation Equipment market can be divided into three types: Task Targeted Automation (TTA), Laboratory Automation (TLA), and Separate Automation Equipment. TTA focuses on automating specific tasks within the laboratory, such as sample preparation or analysis. TLA involves full automation of the entire laboratory workflow, from sample input to data output. Separate Automation Equipment refers to individual pieces of automation equipment, such as liquid handling systems or robotic arms, that can be integrated into a larger automated system.</p></p>
