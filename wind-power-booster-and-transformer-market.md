<p><p>The wind power booster and transformer market primarily consists of two types: dry transformers and oil transformers. Dry transformers are environmentally friendly and require minimal maintenance, making them suitable for renewable energy applications. They operate efficiently without the use of liquid insulating materials. Conversely, oil transformers utilize transformer oil for cooling and insulation, allowing for higher power ratings and better thermal performance. Both types are essential in enhancing power quality and ensuring effective transmission in wind energy systems.</p></p>
