<p><p>The Self-Powered Relays market is categorized into three main types: static, numerical, and auxiliary. Static relays operate based on fixed parameters and provide consistent performance. Numerical relays, on the other hand, use digital technology for advanced functionalities and precise measurements, enhancing reliability and flexibility. Auxiliary relays serve supportive roles, often controlling secondary circuits or ensuring system stability. Together, these relay types optimize electrical systems, improve automation, and contribute to enhanced safety and operational efficiency in various applications.</p></p>
