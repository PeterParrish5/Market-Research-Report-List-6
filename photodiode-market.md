<p><p>The photodiode market is primarily dominated by two types: silicon photodiodes and InGaAs photodiodes. Silicon photodiodes are widely used for general light detection, benefiting from their cost-effectiveness and efficient response in visible light applications. In contrast, InGaAs photodiodes are specialized for infrared detection, excelling in high-speed communication and imaging systems due to their sensitivity to wavelengths above 800 nm. Both types cater to diverse industries, including telecommunications, consumer electronics, and environmental monitoring, driving innovation and market growth.</p></p>
