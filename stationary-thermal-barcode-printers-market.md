<p><strong>The Stationary Thermal Barcode Printers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Thermal</li><li>Thermal Transfer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Stationary thermal barcode printers primarily utilize two technologies: direct thermal and thermal transfer. Direct thermal printers create images by selectively heating coated thermal paper, resulting in a quick, cost-effective printing solution, ideal for labels with shorter lifespan requirements. In contrast, thermal transfer printers utilize a ribbon to transfer ink onto the substrate, producing durable, long-lasting prints suitable for labels exposed to abrasion or environmental factors. Both technologies cater to various industries, depending on label durability and printing needs.</p></p>
