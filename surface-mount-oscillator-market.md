<p><ul><li>SMD Oscillator</li><li>SMT Oscillator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The surface mount oscillator market is primarily categorized into two types: SMD (Surface Mount Device) oscillators and SMT (Surface Mount Technology) oscillators. SMD oscillators are compact components that can be mounted directly onto the surface of circuit boards, offering space efficiency and reliability. SMT oscillators, while similar, emphasize the manufacturing process that facilitates high-density packaging and automated assembly. Both types are integral to modern electronics, providing stable frequency signals for various applications, including communication, computing, and consumer devices.</p></p>
