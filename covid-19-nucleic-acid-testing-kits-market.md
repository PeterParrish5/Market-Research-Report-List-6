<p><p>The COVID-19 nucleic acid testing kits market includes various types such as Fluorescent PCR and Thermostatic Amplification Chips. Fluorescent PCR utilizes fluorescent dyes to detect viral RNA during the polymerase chain reaction process, offering high sensitivity and specificity. In contrast, Thermostatic Amplification Chips employ isothermal amplification technology, enabling rapid and efficient nucleic acid detection at constant temperatures, minimizing equipment complexity. Both methods play crucial roles in ensuring timely diagnosis and effective management of COVID-19 outbreaks.</p></p>
