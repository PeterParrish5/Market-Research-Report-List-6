<p><ul><li>Transmission Lines</li><li>Waveguides</li><li>Optical Fibers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Transmission lines, waveguides, and optical fibers are three main types of telecommunications cables used in the market. Transmission lines carry electrical signals from one point to another, while waveguides guide electromagnetic waves through a hollow structure. Optical fibers use light pulses to transmit data over long distances with high speed and minimal signal loss. Each type of cable offers unique advantages in terms of efficiency, bandwidth, and signal quality, allowing telecommunications providers to choose the most suitable option based on their specific needs.</p></p>
