<p><p>The autonomous shuttle sensors market comprises three main types: camera sensors, radar sensors, and lidar sensors. Camera sensors utilize visual data for object detection and classification, enabling the shuttle to navigate safely. Radar sensors use radio waves to track the speed and position of nearby objects, providing reliable performance in various weather conditions. Lidar sensors emit laser beams to create detailed 3D maps of the environment, allowing for precise spatial awareness. Together, these sensor types enhance the safety and efficiency of autonomous shuttles.</p></p>
