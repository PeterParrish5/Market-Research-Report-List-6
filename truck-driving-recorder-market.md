<p><p>The truck driving recorder market is divided into two main types: integrated and portable. Integrated driving recorders are built directly into the vehicle's system, providing seamless data collection and real-time monitoring. These systems often offer advanced features like GPS tracking and driver behavior analysis. Portable recorders, on the other hand, are standalone devices that can be easily installed or moved between vehicles. They offer flexibility and can be used for various applications, making them ideal for fleet managers and independent drivers.</p></p>
