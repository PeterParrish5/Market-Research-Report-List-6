<p><p>The discrete semiconductors market comprises various components with distinct applications. Diodes allow current to flow in one direction, essential for rectification and signal processing. MOSFETs (Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor Field-Effect Transistors) are used for efficient switching and amplification in power management and digital circuits. IGBTs (Insulated Gate Bipolar Transistors) combine qualities of both MOSFETs and bipolar transistors, making them ideal for medium to high-power applications like motor drives. Other components include rectifiers, transistors, and optoelectronic devices, catering to diverse electronic needs.</p></p>
