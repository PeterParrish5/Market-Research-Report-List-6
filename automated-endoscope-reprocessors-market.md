<p><p>Automated Endoscope Reprocessors (AERs) are crucial for ensuring the safe and efficient cleaning and disinfection of endoscopic instruments. The market is divided into two main types: Single Chamber and Multi Chamber AERs. Single Chamber AERs operate within a single compartment, streamlining the cleaning process but limiting capacity. In contrast, Multi Chamber AERs have distinct compartments for different stages of cleaning and disinfection, allowing for batch processing of multiple endoscopes simultaneously, improving efficiency and workflow in medical facilities.</p></p>
