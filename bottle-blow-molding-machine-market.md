<p><p>Bottle blow molding machines are categorized into two main types: semi-automatic and fully automatic machines. Semi-automatic machines require some manual involvement in the bottle production process, such as moving the preforms and removing the finished bottles. On the other hand, fully automatic machines are more advanced and capable of completing the entire bottle production process without any manual intervention. Both types of machines cater to different market needs, with semi-automatic machines being more cost-effective for small-scale production and fully automatic machines suitable for large-scale production operations.</p></p>
