Lower body exoskeletons support leg movement, enhancing mobility for individuals with lower limb impairments or aiding in rehabilitation. Upper body exoskeletons assist arm and shoulder functions, benefiting workers in industrial settings or those recovering from upper body injuries. Full body exoskeletons integrate both upper and lower supports, providing comprehensive assistance for various applications, including mobility enhancement, rehabilitation, and physical labor support.</p></p>
