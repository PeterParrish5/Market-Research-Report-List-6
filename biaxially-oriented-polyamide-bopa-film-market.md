<p><ul><li>Sequential</li><li>Simultaneous</li><li>Blown</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Biaxially Oriented Polyamide (BOPA) film market consists of three primary processing types: sequential, simultaneous, and blown. In the sequential method, the film is stretched in one direction first and then the other, enhancing clarity and strength. The simultaneous approach stretches the film in both directions simultaneously, optimizing durability and barrier properties. The blown method produces tubes of film by blowing hot polymer, resulting in excellent mechanical properties and thickness uniformity. Each type offers distinct advantages for diverse applications.</p></p>
