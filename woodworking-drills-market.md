<p><p>The woodworking drills market includes various types of drill bits, each designed for specific applications. Forstner drill bits create flat-bottomed holes with clean edges, ideal for making precise holes in wood. Auger drill bits feature a spiral design, allowing deep and wide holes, ideal for boring into thick materials. Spade drill bits, with their flat blades, quickly remove material for larger holes but may sacrifice precision. Other types encompass specialized bits for unique woodworking tasks, catering to diverse project needs.</p></p>
