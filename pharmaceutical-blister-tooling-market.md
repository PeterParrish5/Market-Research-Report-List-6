<p><p>The pharmaceutical blister tooling market encompasses the design and manufacturing of blister packs used for medication packaging. Thermoforming involves heating plastic sheets to create customized blisters that securely hold tablets or capsules, providing visibility and protection. Both methods enhance drug safety, extend shelf life, and offer patient-friendly dispensing solutions within the pharmaceutical industry.</p></p>
